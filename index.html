About Me


I am a third-year PhD candidate at the University of Amsterdam's IRLab, passionate about Natural Language Processing (NLP) and user-centric evaluation of conversational systems. My research focuses on incorporating valuable user perspectives into the development of these systems. I have extensive experience in evaluating conversational AI, specifically concentrating on user-centric evaluation of and with large language models (LLMs). As an active member of MasakhaneNLP, I actively promote NLP research for low-resource African languages.

Research Interests

  • User-centric evaluation of conversational systems
  • Evaluation of and with large language models (LLMs)
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) for low-resource African languages
