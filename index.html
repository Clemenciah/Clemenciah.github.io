About Me

I am Clemencia Siro, a PhD candidate at the University of Amsterdam under the supervision of Maarten de Rijke. My research focus is on the evaluation of conversational systems, specifically from the user's perspective. I aim to improve user experience in their interaction with AI systems. I am also interested on how to leverage LLMs for evaluation and studying how factors affecting humans in system evaluation, affect LLMs when used as evaluators. Apart from that I am an active member of Masakhane where I take part in promoting NLP for Low-resource African languages.

Research Interests

  • User-centric evaluation of conversational systems
  • Evaluation of and with large language models
  • NLP for low-resource African languages
